HOBBIES

READING

Reading also improves your cultural literacy, making it easier to find shared conversation points with others. The more you read, the more ideas you have in your head and the more ways you can find to connect with the people around you.

MUSIC

Music has the ability to change your mood completely, depending on what you are playing or listening to. Music can make you feel anything ranging from happy or excited to sad or angry. Engaging in music releases dopamine which is known as a feel-good chemical and causes us to experience emotions such as happiness and joy. Listening to and playing music provides us with similar bursts of exhilaration that we would get from sex, certain drugs and eating a big piece of chocolate..
